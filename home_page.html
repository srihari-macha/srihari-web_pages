FAQ

How do I book an order with customfurnish.com?

Ans: You can book an order with us online by clicking on the BOOK NOW button and furnishing all the mandatory Information & files

Is the booking amount refundable?

Ans: If you are opting for the Design only package, then the amount you pay is the design fee and not refundable. However if you select the Design and Exeute package,
the amount you pay at the time of booking the order will be absorbed in the total project cost.

Can I switch the package I select at a later point?

Ans: You are free to switch from one package to another at any time if you decide to switch from Design only to Design & Execute, your booking amount will be absorbed in
the total project cost. However, if you sign-up for Design & Execute package and later decide to avail only our design services, we'll not be providing you the Design, BOM &
Engineering drawings.
